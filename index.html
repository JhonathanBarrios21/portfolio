Publications

Selected academic and professional publications.

Topological Descriptors in Gait Foot Clearance Pattern Analysis as a Complementary Framework for Differential Diagnosis in Parkinsonism

Journal of Applied Statistics, 2025 — submitted

Applies topological descriptors to gait foot-clearance time series for differential diagnosis in parkinsonism, comparing robustness and accuracy against conventional ML approaches.

Evaluating the Impact of Dataset Size on Classical and Topological Feature Extraction for Gait Classification

7th International Applied Mathematics, Modelling and Simulation Conference (AMMS 2025), ACM - In Press, 2025

Compares classical preprocessing and TDA-based feature extraction for gait classification. Persistence Landscapes with CatBoost achieved AUC=0.943, showing TDA's strength in small-data scenarios.

Neurological Disease Classification Based on Gait Analysis Through Transformation-Based Multiple Linear Regression Normalization

Congress of the Portuguese Statistical Society (Springer Nature), 2021, pp. 381–392

Applied transformed MLR normalization, PCA and ML (RF, SVM, MLP) to distinguish PD, NPH and controls; SHAP explained feature importance before/after normalization.

Solar Radiation Forecasting: A Study Case in the Colombian Caribbean Region

Congress of the Portuguese Statistical Society (Springer Nature), 2021, pp. 199–210

Compares SARIMA and ML models (SVM, Regression Tree) for monthly/daily solar radiation prediction; ML outperforms SARIMA in short-term forecasting for energy planning.

Impact of Variable Transformations on Multiple Regression Models for Enhancing Gait Normalization

ICoMS '23: 6th International Conference on Mathematics and Statistics, 2023, pp. 103–107

Shows that transforming predictors in MLR normalization better decouples anthropometrics from gait variables, improving model fit and comparability across subjects.

Diffraction efficiency of a double-pass acousto-optic modulation system

Óptica Pura y Aplicada, Vol. 54(1), 2021

Characterizes single and double-pass AOM configurations, optimizing RF and optical setups to improve diffraction efficiency in laboratory applications.

Characterization and optimization of the laser diffracted by an acoustic-optical modulator in the infrared region

Journal of Physics: Conference Series, 2019, Vol. 1219(1):012006

Optimizes acousto-optic modulator efficiency and beam profiles under frequency modulation, detailing adjustments for IR laser spectroscopy applications.
